How to Join Joy

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Visit Our Website

Start by visiting our homepage. You'll find all the necessary information and resources to get started with your mental well-being journey.

2. Sign Up

Click the "Sign Up" button and fill in your personal details like username, email, and password. Don't worry, your data is secure!

3. Verify Your Email

Once you sign up, we will send you a verification email. Follow the link to confirm your email and activate your account.

4. Log In and Set Your Goals

After logging in, you can set your personal goals and start tracking your mental well-being progress. You can also explore various resources and participate in events.

5. Start Your Journey

Once you’ve completed these steps, you’ll be all set to start engaging with the Joy community. We are here to support you every step of the way!
